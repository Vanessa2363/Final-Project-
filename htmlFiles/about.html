    What is Shop Small?

    Shop Small is a website designed to help people find small businesses within the city of Los Angeles. The site provides a variety of small businesses ranging from clothing, restaurants, jewellry/accessories, etc. with detailed descriptions!

    How do you use Shop Small?

    To use the Shop Small website, you can either click on the images on the home page or the shops button in the navigation bar to navigate through the different types of small business. You can also use to navigation bar to check out our about page and special maps feature.

    Who should use Shop Small?

    Anyone can use the Shop Small website! However, the site is mostly targeted towards millenials and GenZ in the Los Angeles area. Los Angeles tourists and travellers are welcome to use Shop Small as a means of enhacing their visit by discovering and visiting many unique small businesses!

    What are the benefits of shopping at small businesses?

    • Shopping small contributes to the local economy as well as the immediate community since small businesses are able to create more job opportunities.
    • Another major reason for shopping small is that you, as a consumer, can decrease the overall consumption of fast food and fast fashion, which are large contributors to environmental destruction and unethical treatments of workers.
    • Shopping small promotes more locally and ethically sourced ingredients whether it be in cloth or food. This leads to higher quality and uniqueness in experience!
